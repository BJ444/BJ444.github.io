Bakery Map in London

Good pastries stimulate discussions. Below are some of the bakeries we love for our reading groups:

Okja!
My favourite plant-based bakery in London. A great variety of options from pistachio swirls to sausage rolls, with no added vegan tax!
Breadary
Their potato, harissa and cheese twist is great. Everything is made fresh on-site too.
Crosstown Doughnuts
All vegan. Everything is good.
